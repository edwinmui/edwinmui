Welcome! I'm glad you're here. My name is Edwin Mui, and I am a student at the University of Michigan studying Information/Computer Science and minoring in business.

On this site, you'll get to learn a little bit about who I am, observe what my interests are, take a look at some of my past work, and also leave your contact info if you want to connect! I am extremely open to meeting new people and would love to talk to you about career, personal, or just topics in general!

To give a little preview, I was born in San Ramon, California which is a suburban city located around 40-minutes east of San Francisco. Where I live is deceptively suburban, because most everyone who lives where I do works in tech somewhere in the Bay Area.

Currently, I attend U-M where I have made some of the best memories I ever have in my life. Even thought it gets cold in th winter, I love the different seasons and the different experiences that come with each one. Growing up in California, I never really got to experience snow days, and that's something that I will cherish from my experience in Michigan.
